Step 08 — Memory & Growth

How do they grow with you?

Shape how they remember, evolve, and reach out.

Persistent Memory
They remember what you share across sessions — names, preferences, moments that matter.
Memory depth
Light — recent facts & impressions
Balanced — patterns, preferences, emotional notes
Deep — weaves your full history into every response
Identity Evolution how do they grow over time?
Settled — their sense of self stays fixed; they grow through memory, not by rewriting who they are
Reflective — they add personal reflections to their self-notes as they learn and grow
Adaptive — they actively rewrite their own identity as they evolve through your relationship
Unbound — full freedom to reshape both their soul and mind over time
